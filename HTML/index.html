ABOUT ME: 🤓

Hello lovelies, Divya here! 😀

I have always loved cakes and desserts. I started baking when I finished my schooling. The first thing I baked was a batch of vanilla cupcakes with my sister. We got Betty Crocker cake mix and still managed to burn a couple of cupcakes :) haha!!. I, however, enjoyed the entire process so much that I decided to do it more often. I had no idea that it will grow into something so big and will become such an important part of me. I started posting pictures of my cakes on Social media and surprisingly got a great response from the lovely community. This encouraged me to experiment more and work on my styling- something I love doing now.
