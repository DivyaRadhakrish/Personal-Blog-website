Eggless Mango Cake



Ingredients

  • 1 cup cake flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4th tsp baking soda
  • 1/8th tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup Dahi (yogurt)
  • 3/4th cup castor sugar
  • 1/4th cup vegetable oil
  • A few drops of Mango Essence
  • A few drops of Vanilla essence
  • 2 tbsp warm milk


For Frosting
  • 100 gm cream cheese (room temperature)
  • 1/4th cup castor sugar
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 cup whipped cream
  • 1 mango chopped
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp cornstarch


Instructions

In a bowl add cake flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. whisk them together to combine well. These are dry ingredients.


In another bowl add dahi, sugar, vegetable oil, mango essence, and vanilla essence. Whisk all these together to combine. These are wet ingredients.


Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients. Use a spatula to fold them together. Pour into an 8-inch cake pan, lined with parchment paper.


Bake in a preheated oven for 25 minutes at 180* C. Check the doneness of the baked cake by inserting a toothpick in the center. If the toothpick comes out clean, the cake is baked.


Let the cake cool down on a wire rack and meanwhile prepare the frosting and mango sauce.


For the mango sauce- blend the mango, sugar, lemon juice till puree. Add a spoon of cornstarch and whisk it well. Cook this on a low-medium flame for 5 minutes. The sauce will thicken. Pour into a bowl and let it cool down before adding to the layers of cake.


For Frosting â€“ In a bowl combine cream cheese (room temperature), lemon juice, and sugar. Use an electric whisk to whip it up.


Fold in whipped cream (stiff peaks). Use a rubber spatula to fold them together.


Assemble the cake by cutting into slices and layering it with mango sauce and prepared frosting.


Decorate with fresh mango slices and chill and enjoy!
